Bowen Yu / 郁博文

Bowen Yu is a Algorithm Expert of Language Technology Lab, Alibaba DAMO Academy. He received his Ph.D. degree from the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2022, under the supervision of Professor Tingwen Liu and Bin Wang. His research interests include Information Extraction and Conventional AI etc. Up to now, He has published several papers in the world top conferences such as WWW, SIGIR, ACL, EMNLP, AAAI and IJCAI.
